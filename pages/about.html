About CleanCo

A family-owned laundry service with a passion for clean clothes and happy customers since 2010.

Our Story

CleanCo was founded in 2010 by the Sharma family with a single washing machine and a big dream. What started as a small neighbourhood laundromat in Mumbai has grown into a full-service laundry and dry-cleaning company trusted by thousands of households across the city.

Our mission is simple: deliver spotlessly clean clothes on time, every time, while treating every garment as if it were our own. We combine modern technology with old-fashioned attention to detail to give you the best results possible.
