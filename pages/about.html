About CleanCo

A family owned laundry service with a passion for clean clothes since 2010.

Our Story

CleanCo was started in 2010 by the Sharma family with just one washing machine and a big dream. What started as a small laundromat has now grown into a full service laundry company trusted by thousands of families.

Our mission is simple - deliver clean clothes on time every time while treating every garment like our own.

We combine modern equipment with old fashioned attention to detail to give you the best results possible.
